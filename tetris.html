Keyboard Handling

Keys:
  • Left Arrow - Move shape left
  • Right Arrow - Move shape right
  • Up Arrow - Rotate shape
  • Down Arrow - Drop shape
  • P - pause / resume game
  • F - faster

Mouse Handling

Clicks:
  • Click over current shape - Rotate shape
  • Click left of current shape - Move shape left
  • Click right of current shape - Move shape right
